# Finite Mobius inversion

Goal: isolate the finite incidence-algebra statements that make cancellation visible before passing to Dirichlet-series/asymptotic language.

Initial anchor:

- divisibility as a finite poset on `{1, ..., N}`;
- zeta matrix `Z(a,b) = 1` when `a | b`;
- Mobius matrix as the inverse of `Z`;
- cancellation as inversion rather than mystery.

Lean target: formalize the finite poset and the zeta/Mobius inverse statement for finite truncations.
